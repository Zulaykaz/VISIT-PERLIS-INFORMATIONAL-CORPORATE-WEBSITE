Culture and Heritage

Explore the rich cultural practices and heritage of Perlis that have been preserved through generations, reflecting the traditions, customs and identity of the local community.

Unique Traditions in Perlis

The Berinai ceremony

The Berinai ceremony is a traditional pre-wedding ritual in Perlis where the bride and groom have henna applied as a symbol of blessing and beauty. It has three stages, Berinai Curi, Berinai Kecil, and Berinai Besar, accompanied by music, dance and prayers according to Malay-Islamic customs.

Gotong-Royong

Gotong royong in Perlis is a community tradition where neighbors come together to clean, repair or organize communal activities, fostering cooperation, unity and mutual help.

Rice Field Taboos

In Perlis, traditional rice field taboos guide farmers to avoid certain actions or behaviors such as stepping into the field with dirty clothes, whistling at night or cutting young plants to ensure good harvests, safety and harmony with nature.

Fishermen’s Taboos

Fishermen in Perlis follow traditional taboos such as avoiding fishing on certain days, not whistling at sea, or not arguing while at sea, to ensure safety, good catch and harmony with nature.

Adat Berendoi

Berendoi is a traditional Malay ceremony in Perlis held for newborn babies, where the baby is placed in a cradle (endoi), family and friends pray for the baby’s health, protection and blessings, often accompanied by songs, lullabies and light celebration.

Death Customs and Tahlil

In Perlis, death customs follow Islamic practices, including funeral preparation, Salat al-Janazah (funeral prayer), tahlil recitations and grave visits. These rites honor the deceased, pray for their soul and strengthen community ties.

Historical Places in Perlis

Historic museum showcasing Perlis’ history and artifacts.

Masjid Alwi

Historic mosque in Kangar, known for its unique architectural style and cultural significance.

Istana Diraja Arau

Royal palace of Perlis’ Sultan, combining traditional Malay and modern architectural elements.

Bangunan Pentadbiran Kangar

Administrative building of Kangar, reflecting colonial-era architecture.

Traditional Dances of Perlis

Canggung Dance is a traditional dance from Perlis that reflects the simple lifestyle and joyful spirit of the local community.
