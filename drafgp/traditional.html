Taste of Perlis: Traditional Cuisine
Pulut Pelam Harumanis is a traditional dessert made with glutinous rice and Perlis Harumanis mango. A seasonal delicacy.
Kuih Serabai is a soft pancake made from rice flour and coconut milk, often served with sweet gravy.
Jeruk Maman is made from fermented maman leaves, eaten as a side dish with rice.
Kuih Karas is a crispy lace-like snack made from rice flour, popular during festive occasions.
Dangai is grated coconut mixed with flour and sugar, baked until crispy. A traditional Perlis treat.
Pulut Sambal is glutinous rice served with spicy sambal, combining soft texture and rich flavor.
Apam Puri is a soft steamed rice cake filled with sweet custard, palm sugar, or coconut.
Pulut Ikan Kering is glutinous rice with dried fish, often accompanied by spicy condiments.
Ringgi/Emping is thin crispy discs made from pounded rice, eaten with sugar or curry.
Udang Sanggul is prawns coated in batter, rolled in thin spring roll pastry, and deep-fried until crispy.
