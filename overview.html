Overview of Perlis

Perlis is located at the northern tip of Peninsular Malaysia, covering an area of about 821 sq km. It is the smallest state in Malaysia with a peaceful environment, rich cultural heritage, limestone hills and unique local traditions.

Its capital, Kangar, features a mix of colonial and modern architecture, while smaller towns offer a glimpse into the calm rural lifestyle and local culture. The state is known for its serene natural landscapes and vibrant cultural events.
